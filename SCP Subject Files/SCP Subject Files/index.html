SCP Foundation Web Application

Welcome to the SCP Foundation's official web application. Here, you will find information on anomalous objects, entities, and phenomena that are contained and protected by the Foundation.

What is the SCP Foundation?

The SCP Foundation is a secret organization dedicated to the containment and study of anomalous objects and entities, referred to as "SCPs." These anomalies may pose a threat to humanity, and the Foundation works tirelessly to ensure they remain secure and classified.
